Fundamental Rights

All citizens are equal before the law, prohibiting discrimination and ensuring equal opportunity.

Guarantees freedom of speech, assembly, association, movement, and profession while ensuring personal liberty.

Prohibits human trafficking, forced labor, and child labor.

Ensures religious freedom and equality of all faiths.

Protects rights of minorities to preserve culture, language, and education.

Allows citizens to approach the Supreme Court or High Court for enforcement of fundamental rights.

Fundamental Duties

Abide by the Constitution and respect national symbols.

Promote spirit of patriotism and uphold unity.

Safeguard forests, lakes, rivers, wildlife, and public property.

Strive for scientific temper and knowledge advancement.

Anti-Corruption Laws

Establishes penalties for bribery of public officials and misuse of authority.

Institutes anti-corruption ombudsmen at central and state levels.

Provides protection for individuals reporting corruption or misconduct.
